New York Style Pizza

Ingredients

For the dough
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 5 cups bread flour and more if needed
For the sauce
  • San Marzano whole tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoon tomato paste
For the cheese
  • 6 ounce whole-milk low moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Grated parmesan for dusting

Steps

  1. Bloom the yeast in a stand mixer bowl with the teaspoon of sugar and 1/4 cup water
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients when it's frothy
  3. Mix with dough hook until a smooth ball comes together
  4. Add additional flour if it's took sticky
  5. Divide dough into four equal parts and roll each into a smooth ball and place each in its well oiled bowl
  6. Age in the refrigerator for at least 24 hours or up to a week
  7. For the sauce, remove tomatoes from their canning liquid and blend until smooth.
  8. Then add the remaining ingredients and mix until smooth.
  9. Place the pizza stone in the oven and preheat it to the maximum temperature for an hour
  10. After pre-heating, stretch the pizza dough and place on a well dusted peel
  11. Top with sauce and dust with the parmesan and then with mozzarella
  12. Bake for 5 to 6 minutes
  13. Rest on the cooling rack before slicing.
