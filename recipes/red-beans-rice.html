Southern Style Red Beans and Rice

Almost every warm weather culture has some version of red beans and rice. It's cheap, easy, tasty and good for you!

Ingredients

  • red beans
  • red onion
  • red bell pepper
  • couple stalks of celery
  • tablespoons of tomato paste
  • chicken stock
  • salt
  • oregano
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • cayenne pepper
  • sugar
  • lemon
  • olive oil
  • rice

Steps

  1. Soak the beans overnight.Adding in some salt helps gives the beans a creamier texture inside the beans
  2. Drain out the soak water
  3. Dice up a red onion, celery and a bell pepper
  4. Add some olive oil to a big pot and dump in the vegetables
  5. Put the stove on high heat and stir constantly
  6. Stir in some tomato paste and quickly add the beans some fresh water
  7. Season with salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, oregano and garlic powder at any point
  8. Stir in some sugar and lemon juice at the very end to make it more vibrant
