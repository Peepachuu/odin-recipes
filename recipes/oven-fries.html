Crispy Oven Fries

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Salt
  • Baking soda
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the potatos into fry-like shapes with even thickness
  2. Boil them for around 5 minutes
  3. Gently drain them and lay them on a pan wide enough to hold them in a single layer
  4. Drizze on enough oil to coat all the pieces
  5. Shake to evenly distribute them
  6. Roast at 450F until they are brown and cripsy.
  7. Season the fries in a bowl with a seasoning mix of your choice
